# SEO That Doesn't Put You to Sleep

SEO. Three letters that make most marketers groan. But it doesn't have to be a nightmare of technical jargon and keyword stuffing. Let's make it simple (and maybe even fun).

## What SEO Actually Is

Think of Google as a really helpful librarian. Your job? Make it easy for the librarian to find your book and recommend it to people asking for it.

That's it. That's SEO.

## The Only 3 Things You Need to Know

### 1. Write What People Are Searching For

Use tools like:
- Google Autocomplete (free!)
- AnswerThePublic (also free!)
- Look at "People also ask" in search results

Then create content that actually answers those questions. Revolutionary, right?

### 2. Make It Readable

- Use headers (like this one!)
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Bullet points for lists
- Images to break up text

Google reads structure. Humans read content. Make both happy.

### 3. Get Other Sites to Mention You

This is the "link building" part, simplified:
- Guest post on relevant blogs
- Create content so good people share it
- Build relationships with other creators

## The One Thing That Actually Matters

**Intent matching.** 

If someone searches "best running shoes," they want comparison content, not your shoe store's homepage. Give people what they're looking for, and Google will reward you.
